Alpha Chapter | Long Beach

Multicultural Fraternity and Non-Profit Organization

In the beautiful setting of southern California, where 365 days of sunshine intertwines with coastal grace and urban landscape, 8 young men gathered together with the goal to bring to life a common apparition. With the vision of bringing young men across varying backgrounds together, linked by the same common values and desires, the brotherhood was born.

Since its initial foundation in 2008, Zion's Mightiest Brotherhood Alpha Chapter has grown to over 90 brothers and has only expanded with its' Lil Sis Program. Although fairly young, the presence of the brotherhood does not go unnoticed, brothers are active participants in the betterment of the community, innovative and forward thinking leaders in their pursuits, and a force of nature collectively. Based in California State University, Long Beach, the Zeta Mu Beta Alpha Chapter continues to thrive and endeavor with the original vision and core values foreseen those many years before.

A Brotherhood Built on Common Cornerstones

UNITY

One Family, united by bonds of brotherhood, with the support of our sisters, we stand together.

STRENGTH

Enduring the adversity we encounter. Instilled with the discipline to persevere until the end.

DIVERSITY

Multi-Cultural and Cognizant, we embrace difference. The power in our combined differences projects us forward.

VIRTUE

Gentlemen in Character and Conduct, embodying the good collectively to make differences individually.

BROTHERHOOD

Fueled by one dream, founded by eight men, and built upon common values, we continue to stand together, strive together, and motivate each other.
