Bros Rush

Check out our Zeta Mu Beta Facebook page and updates from our rush chairs on Facebook about future rush events every Fall and Spring semester.

We rush for newer brothers to join the family every semester and are actively present in California State University, Long Beach across a diverse array of organizations. We are open and have a great network for you to add on, so join our brotherhood and check out our rush events to see if it is for you.

Feel free to contact any of the active brothers or send us an email to inquire more about the logistics.

Queens Rush

Check out our Zeta Mu Beta Facebook page and updates from our queen's chairs on Facebook about future rush events every Fall semester.

We are welcoming all you lovely ladies to learn more about and potentially join our tight-knit family as little sises.

Feel free to contact any of the active brothers or reach out to fellow queens in our family to learn more about what it means to be a queen in Zeta Mu Beta.

Catch us flyering around the beginning of the Fall semesters in California State University, Long Beach.
